How to Trade Economic Events

  • High Impact: Reduce position size, use wider stops
  • Medium Impact: Be cautious around release times
  • Low Impact: Usually safe to trade through
  • Wait 5-15 minutes after news release for volatility to settle
  • Always check consensus forecasts vs actual results

Event Impact Guide

High Impact Events
Can cause significant market moves (50+ pips). Trade with caution.

Medium Impact Events
Moderate market movement (20-50 pips). Adjust position sizes.

Low Impact Events
Minor market effects (<20 pips). Normal trading conditions.
